About Manga Bookstore

Our Vision

Farid Damania, Heet Dhandhukia, and Harsh Mahadik are united by their shared passion for manga. Their vision for Manga Bookstore is to provide a haven for manga lovers and introduce newcomers to the captivating world of Japanese comics. Their goal is to create a space where fans of all ages and backgrounds can come together to explore, discover, and share their love for manga.

A Manga Lover's Paradise

At Manga Bookstore, every aspect of the store is carefully designed to create an immersive and welcoming experience. The store's interior is adorned with vibrant manga artwork, and the shelves are stocked with a diverse collection of manga titles, from classic series to the latest releases. Customers can find manga in various genres, including action, romance, fantasy, and much more.

Community and Events

Farid, Heet, and Harsh understand that the love for manga goes beyond just reading. That's why they have created a space where manga enthusiasts can connect, engage, and build a community. The store regularly hosts events, book readings, cosplay contests, and discussions, giving customers the opportunity to meet like-minded individuals and share their passion for manga.

A Place for All Ages

Manga Bookstore is designed to be an inclusive space for manga lovers of all ages. Whether you're a seasoned manga veteran or a newcomer looking to explore this fascinating world, you'll find something to suit your interests and level of familiarity. The store's knowledgeable staff is always on hand to offer recommendations and help you discover your next favorite series.

Beyond Manga

Manga Bookstore isn't just a place for books. It's a hub for all things manga-related. Customers can find a wide range of manga merchandise, from posters and figures to apparel and accessories. The store is a true celebration of Japanese pop culture.
